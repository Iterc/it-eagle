Now you can watch YouTube on Apple Watch (suddenly someone really wanted to)

A free app WatchTube for watchOS 8 and iOS 13 and later has been released for Apple Watch.

It allows you to watch YouTube directly on your smart watch. There are four sections,
as in the mobile application: Home, Search, Library and Settings.

Recommendations are shown on the main page. There is support for subtitles, you need
content can be searched via keyboard or dictation. Also available to users is
the ability to share videos using a QR code.

The app supports text resizing. Sound output to AirPods or
through the watch's built-in speakers. However, there is one “but” - it is not yet possible to connect to
your account. The developers promise to later add the "Audio" function for
listening to video in the background.
