Xiaomi Mi Band 7 and Xiaomi Mi Band 7 NFC received
many new features in the first firmware update

Now users can change the vibration intensity on the fitness tracker itself

At the end of last month, Xiaomi released the Xiaomi Mi Band 7 fitness bracelet, which has already sold out
with a circulation of more than a million units. And now he received the first firmware update.

Xiaomi announced the release of an update for the Xiaomi Mi Band 7 and Xiaomi Mi Band 7 NFC models.
The firmware passes under the number 1.16.0.7, includes many fixes and several new features.

With the new update, for the first time in the series data devices, Xiaomi Mi Band 7 users can finally
change the vibration intensity on the fitness tracker itself. Until now, this has been possible
only via an app from a smartphone. It is unknown if this feature will be available for models
the previous generation.

In addition, the built-in watch faces have been improved. Clicking on the component will open
corresponding screen. For example, pressing the pulse number on the watch face will open the page
heart rate data. The latest new feature is the addition of fitness reminders. It is intended
to encourage owners and encourage them to achieve their goals.

Speaking of bugs, the new update contains fixes for occasional restart issues,
built-in text alignment of watch faces in the alarm section and problems with brightness after
exit from the payment page (NFC version). In addition, the update also optimizes
system performance.
